由于开卷机和整平机的一体化，得以节省空间；加之整平机可方便的调节工作角度，故可根据现有空间灵活设置。
By combining the uncoiler and straightener ,we have succeeded in creating this new space saving model. The position of straightener is easily changeable for more flexible machine layout.
